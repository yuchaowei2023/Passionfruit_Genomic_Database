Welcome to the Passionfruit Genomics Network (PGN)

Passiflora edulis, an important Passiflora fruit, and popularly known as passionfruits. Passionfruit not only have high nutritional and nutraceutical values but also possess delicious taste which makes them very popular. Owing to the increased interest in the agriculture value of passionfruit, this study was dedicated to the development of genomic resources for genetics and breeding programs. The current release of PGN employs the first comprehensively annotated passionfruits plant genome (Passiflora edulis) as framework, and incorporates with abundant well‐organized transcriptomes (different tissues and environmental stresses in Passiflora edulis), diverse gene expression patterns (across species, tissue, stress, and hormone treatment).
