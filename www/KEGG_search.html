KEGG Assignment of Passionfruit Genes

Plant KEGG Orthology, which are a simple version of KEGG Orthology were assigned to proteins encoded in the Passionfruit plant genome by using BLASTP to search against Arabidopsis thaliana proteome, TAIR 10 Genome Release.

Examples:
K15601 will find all genes containing the KEGG Ortholog:K15601 in the passionfruit plant genome database.
03036 will find all genes containing the KEGG Ontology：03036 in the passionfruit plant genome database.
